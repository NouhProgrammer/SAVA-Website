Saudia Arabia - An Overview

Saudi Arabian Virtual Airlines, commonly known as Saudia VA, is a premier virtual airline established on May 11, 2025. Founded by visionary CEO Rauf Ibrahimov, Saudia VA aims to bring the passion of real-world aviation to the virtual skies. The virtual airline focuses on delivering a realistic and immersive flying experience for flight simulation enthusiasts worldwide, with a special emphasis on professionalism, safety, and community.
Saudia VA operates a modern fleet that mirrors the real Saudi Arabian Airlines, with pilots trained on accurate procedures and routes, especially within the Middle East region and beyond. The airline prides itself on its active and friendly pilot community, regular events, and commitment to high-quality virtual operations.
In addition, Saudia VA offers comprehensive training programs designed for new VATSIM members and beginners. This training helps newcomers learn the basics of flying, communication with ATC, and airline procedures, ensuring they can fly confidently and safely within the virtual skies.
